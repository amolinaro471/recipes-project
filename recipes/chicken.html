Baked Chicken

Description

An italian herb encrusted chicken seared then baked to perfection!

Ingredients

  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • A pinch of italian herbs
  • 1/2 pound of chicken breast

Steps

  1. Place oil into a fry pan and place on medium heat.
  2. Place oven to 400 degreees F
  3. Place chicken breast onto hot fry pan.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and herbs to side facing up.
  5. Flip to other side after approximately 4 minutes
  6. Repeat step 3
  7. Once browned on both sides, take off fry pan and place on baking sheet.
  8. Place into oven and cook for 15 minutes or internal temp of 165
  9. Enjoy!!!!
