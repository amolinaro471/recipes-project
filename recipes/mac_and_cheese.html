Mac and Cheese

Description

A Deliciously cheesy meal for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • A pinch of salt
  • A knob of butter
  • cup of milk
  • 1 box of mac and cheese

Steps

  1. Follow instructions on the box
  2. Once pasta is cooked fully, drain the water
  3. Add the cup of milk, along with the butter and pinch of salt
  4. Add in the cheese sauce and stir until velvet consistency
  5. Enjoy!
