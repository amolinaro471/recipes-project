Italian Sausage

Description

An Italian Sausage seared and baked to perfection!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sausage
  • 2 cans of beer
  • 1 cut up green pepper
  • 1 cut up onion

Steps

  1. Dump the cans of beer into a saucepan and boil
  2. Once the beer is boiling, place sausages in for 10-15 minutes
  3. Take sausages out of the beer and pat dry with paper towel
  4. Place sausages on baking sheet and then into a preheated oven at 350F
  5. Wait 10 minutes for the sausages to finish up
  6. Caramelize the onions and peppers in a medium fry pan
  7. Place sausages, peppers and onions all on a nice sausage roll
  8. Enjoy!
